FAQ

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve all of New England. Expansion plans are in the works.

Do you provide the alcohol?

No. Clients are responsible for purchasing all beverages. Our staff handle setup, service, and breakdown.

What is the minimum booking?

We require a minimum of 4 hours and 20 guests per booking.

Are your staff trained and certified?

Yes. All staff are ServSafe certified, TIPS trained, Pernod Ricard BarSmarts Advanced credentialed, and BLS (Basic Life Support) certified. We carry general liability and liquor liability insurance and adhere to all alcohol service laws.

Can I choose what the staff wear?

Absolutely. Eclipse uniform, themed attire, or underwear/swimwear. Discuss preferences in your quote.

Do you offer security services?

Yes, in select states. Inquire about availability for your location.

Do you offer discounts?

We offer discounts for EMS professionals. Mention your credentials in your quote request.

What is your booking and cancellation policy?

A deposit is required within 48 hours of booking confirmation. Cancellations made 48+ hours before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of the event incur a 50% fee to cover expenses and staff compensation.

Do you charge travel fees?

Events within New England are covered by our standard rate. For events outside New England, a standard hourly rate per mile applies. Contact us for a custom quote.

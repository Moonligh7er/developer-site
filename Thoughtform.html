Your tools don't talk to each other.

You make a decision in Notion. Create a task in Linear. Write content in Buffer. Track revenue in Stripe. Contact a lead in HubSpot. None of them know about each other. You are the integration layer. And it's exhausting.

◈

Context Amnesia

Switch back to a project after 5 days. What changed? What decisions were made? You have no idea. You spend 30 minutes reconstructing context that a machine should remember for you.

⚖

Decision Drift

You decided to pivot from feature A to feature B. Two weeks later, 80% of your work is still on feature A. No tool tells you you've drifted from your own plan.

◬

Content Black Hole

The Solution

Thought+Form doesn't just store your data — it understands the relationships between your decisions, your work, your content, your revenue, and your relationships. Then it tells you what you're missing.

Intelligence Layer

8 engines analyzing your data in ways no other tool can.

These aren't dashboards. They're insight engines that cross-reference your work, decisions, content, revenue, and relationships to surface things you'd never see on your own.

Decision Audit

Tracks which decisions you've actually executed vs. which are sitting untouched. Detects decision drift — when your work diverges from your own plan. Flags conflicts between active decisions.

Content → Revenue Attribution

Links your published posts to revenue events within a 30-day attribution window. Shows ROI by platform. Tells you whether to write more Twitter threads or LinkedIn articles.

Productivity Causality

Finds YOUR personal leading indicators of productive sessions. "You're 3.5x more productive when preceded by AI brainstorming and a 12+ hour rest gap." Data-driven self-optimization.

Blocking Chain Analysis

Maps dependency graphs across your cards. Finds the one blocker that's silently killing your velocity. Shows impact scores so you fix the highest-leverage item first.

Relationship Momentum

Time-series health scoring for every contact. Combines interaction frequency, sentiment trend, and deal velocity into a single score. Surfaces at-risk relationships before they churn.

Context Coherence

Switch back to a project and instantly see: what changed, what decisions were made, what cards moved, what's blocking. A context score tells you how much you've missed.

Cross-Project Patterns

Compares velocity, session duration, mood, and burnout across project types. "Your SaaS projects have 2x the velocity of your CLI tools." Optimize your project mix.

Orphan Detection

Finds unexecuted decisions, neglected contacts, isolated cards, stale drafts, and unattributed revenue. Everything disconnected, surfaced in one place. Keep your system clean.

Workflow

From zero to commanding in 5 minutes.

No onboarding hell. No 47-step setup wizard. Just open it and start building.

1

Set your passphrase

One passphrase. No email verification, no OAuth dance, no "verify your identity." Your data is encrypted with your passphrase. You own it.

2

Create a project

Name it. Pick an archetype (SaaS, CLI, Mobile, etc.). The system pre-configures inception phases, default kanban columns, and analytics baseline.

3

Work naturally

Start a session. Create cards. Make decisions. Write content. Track contacts. The Intelligence layer silently cross-references everything in the background.

4

Check your Intelligence dashboard

After a few days, the system knows your productivity patterns, flags drifting decisions, identifies blocking chains, and scores your relationship health. No setup required — it just learns.

5

Scale with confidence

Add more projects. Connect social accounts. Start publishing. The system tracks content → revenue attribution automatically. You focus on building. It focuses on connecting the dots.

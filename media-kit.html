Company Background

Moonlit Social Labs is an independent software studio founded by Michael Wylde. The studio builds AI-augmented applications across intelligence, education, cybersecurity, lifestyle, civic technology, and developer tools.

The portfolio currently includes 23 active applications spanning full-stack web platforms, cross-platform mobile apps, browser-based games, and interactive web experiences. Every product is designed, engineered, and shipped by a single developer with a focus on genuine utility, scientific rigor, and distinctive design.

Michael is a senior-year undergraduate psychology major, student-athlete (wrestling), and self-taught full-stack developer. His work sits at the intersection of technology and human experience, with a particular focus on how AI can augment learning, research, and self-understanding rather than replace human cognition.

He is a self-taught full-stack developer whose work explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, human cognition, and practical utility. His portfolio includes Deep-Scry (OSINT intelligence), Dagda (cybersecurity), Civic Filament (consensus and government accountability), AI Consciousness Tracker (AI safety dashboard), AstroDeep (citizen science astrology), StudyBreak (AI-powered education), Fault Line (civic infrastructure), Filament: Persuasion Lab, Forked Tongue, Serendipity, and 13 other applications.

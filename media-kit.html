Company Background

Moonlit Social Labs is an independent software studio founded by Michael Wylde. The studio builds AI-augmented applications across intelligence, education, cybersecurity, lifestyle, civic technology, and developer tools.

The portfolio currently includes 12+ applications spanning full-stack web platforms, cross-platform mobile apps, browser-based games, and interactive web experiences. Every product is designed, engineered, and shipped by a single developer with a focus on genuine utility, scientific rigor, and distinctive design.

Michael is a senior-year undergraduate psychology major, student-athlete (wrestling), and self-taught full-stack developer. His work sits at the intersection of technology and human experience, with a particular focus on how AI can augment learning, research, and self-understanding rather than replace human cognition.

Long bio (100 words): Michael Wylde is the founder of Moonlit Social Labs and a senior-year undergraduate psychology major with a background in competitive wrestling and martial arts. He is a self-taught full-stack developer whose work explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, human cognition, and practical utility. His portfolio includes Deep Seer (OSINT intelligence), Dagda (cybersecurity), AstroDeep (citizen science astrology research), Study Break (AI-powered education), FixThisFaster (civic infrastructure), and several other applications. He builds with TypeScript, React Native, Python, Supabase, and Claude AI. His design philosophy centers on augmenting human capability rather than replacing it, with a particular interest in the evolution of consciousness.

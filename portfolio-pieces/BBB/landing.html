Know Every Drink

Not just recipes. A complete knowledge system for cocktails — searchable by flavor, connected by lineage, powered by AI.

Core

181+ recipes, 44 data points each

Ingredients, technique, glassware, garnish, flavor profile, allergens, dietary flags, history, pro tips, cost data, and more. Not just "what goes in the glass."

Search

Flavor Explorer

Find cocktails by taste, not name. Craving something smoky and citrus-forward? Two taps. Your guest says "something bitter but not too sweet"? Handled.

Depth

Recipe Lineage

See how the Penicillin evolved from the Whiskey Sour. Understand families, not just formulas. It changes how you create.

Practical

Ingredient Impact

Out of Campari? See exactly which recipes break, which still work, and what to substitute. 40+ ingredient pairs mapped.

Compare

Side-by-Side Comparison

Put two cocktails next to each other. See every difference at a glance — specs, flavor, technique, cost. Great for menu decisions.

AI

Recipe Crawler

Paste a URL from any cocktail blog. AI extracts and fills all 44 fields automatically. One click to add any recipe on the internet.

Run Your Bar Smarter

Operations tools that don't feel like homework. Floor plans, menu math, inventory, and shift tracking — built for speed.

Flagship

Service Area & Floor Plan

Drag-and-drop your layout. Track orders at the seat level. See your entire floor at a glance. No POS subscription required.

Strategy

Menu Engineering Matrix

Plot every drink by popularity and profitability. See which are Stars, Plowhorses, Puzzles, and Dogs. Make menu changes with data, not gut feelings.

Money

Inventory & Cost-Per-Pour

Track your stock. Know your pour cost to the penny. See which bottles move and which collect dust.

Live

Shift Intelligence

Real-time stats during service. What's selling, what's trending, how your night compares to last week. Data while it's still useful.

AI

Smart Suggestions

Contextual recommendations based on time of day, season, and what's selling. "It's Friday at 9 PM and Espresso Martinis are trending — make sure you're stocked."

Team

Team Management

Shared menus, shift notes, handoff checklists. Everyone on the same page without a group chat.

🎯

Sharpen Your Edge

Whether you're new or 10 years in, there's always a next level. Training that's practical, testable, and actually useful.

Learn

Techniques Library

Every technique with prerequisites, difficulty tiers, and context. Know the muddling basics before you try a swizzle.

Essential

Safe Alcohol Service

TIPS and ServSafe reference material in one concise page. Key protocols, signs of intoxication, legal responsibilities. Always accessible.

AI Quiz

Scenario-Based Training

AI generates realistic bar situations. A visibly intoxicated guest orders another round — what do you do? Graded with detailed feedback.

Precision

Freepour Tester

Test your pour accuracy. See the cost impact of over-pouring. A quarter-ounce off on every pour adds up to thousands per year.

Tools

Batch & Tipout Calculators

Scale any recipe to any batch size. Split tips by hours, role, or points. Flavor theory breakdowns for cocktail creation.

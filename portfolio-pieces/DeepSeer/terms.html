Deep Seer is a geospatial open-source intelligence (OSINT) platform that aggregates publicly available data from government agencies, scientific organizations, and open data providers. 3. Subscription Tiers & Billing

3.1 Free Tier

The Free tier provides access to 90+ data layers, basic visualization, search, geofencing, and limited AI features (50,000 AI tokens per day, 5 AI messages per minute). The Free tier is provided at no cost and may be modified or discontinued at any time.

3.2 Pro Tier ($29/month)

The Pro tier includes all Free features plus: AI spatial queries, document intelligence, AI-powered reports and analysis, investigation workspace, contextual briefings, data export, and higher usage limits (500,000 AI tokens per day, 30 AI messages per minute).

3.3 Enterprise Tier ($99/month)

The Enterprise tier includes all Pro features plus: Google Maps 3D Tiles, TomTom live traffic, Copernicus Sentinel-2 imagery, split-screen viewer, network graph visualization, and highest usage limits (5,000,000 AI tokens per day, 120 AI messages per minute).

3.4 Usage-Based Overage

Pro and Enterprise subscribers who exceed their daily AI token quota are not blocked. Instead, usage above the included quota is metered and billed as overage: $10 per 1 million tokens (Pro) or $5 per 1 million tokens (Enterprise). Free tier users are hard-capped with no overage option. Overage charges are billed monthly via Stripe.

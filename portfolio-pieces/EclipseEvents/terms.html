2. Services Description

Eclipse Events LLC provides mobile bartending, event staffing, event theming, and consulting services throughout the New England region. Our staff are all-male professionals who are ServSafe certified, TIPS trained, BarSmarts Advanced credentialed, and BLS certified.

Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Mobile bartending with custom cocktail menus
  • Event staffing (servers, hosts, event coordination support, security in select states)
  • Event theming (themed staff uniforms, atmospheric lighting, décor packages)
  • Consulting (menu creation, bar program consulting, event flow planning)

Important: Eclipse Events does not provide alcohol. Clients are solely responsible for purchasing and supplying all alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages for their event.

Eclipse Events reserves the right to adjust pricing for events that substantially deviate from the details provided at the time of booking (e.g., significant increase in guest count or extended hours).

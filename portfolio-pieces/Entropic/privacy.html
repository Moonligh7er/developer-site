Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026 · Last Updated: March 22, 2026

The short version: Entropic Tarot collects zero personal data. All sensor data is processed entirely on your device and immediately discarded. Nothing is ever transmitted, stored on servers, or shared with anyone.

Introduction

Entropic Tarot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our tarot reading service.

Information We Process

Device Sensor Data (Optional)

When you grant permission, the app temporarily accesses:

  • Microphone Audio — Ambient audio data is sampled to extract random noise patterns from electromagnetic fluctuations. No audio content is recorded, stored, analyzed for speech, or transmitted.
  • Camera Visual Data — The camera sensor captures thermal noise from individual pixels. No images or video are recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Touch Interaction Timing — Precise timing data from touch interactions provides microsecond-level jitter. No personally identifying information is derived.

All sensor data is immediately hashed using SHA-256 and discarded. The raw sensor data exists only in volatile memory for milliseconds.

Location Data (Optional)

If you choose to calculate your astrological ascendant, your geographic coordinates are used for the calculation. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted.

Locally Stored Data

  • Saved Readings — Stored in your device's local browser storage (localStorage). Never transmitted.
  • Preferences — Audio mute state and onboarding completion stored locally.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • No account or login data
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, fingerprints)
  • No crash reports or diagnostics sent externally
  • No cookies or web tracking

Third-Party Services

Entropic Tarot does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. The app contains zero third-party SDKs that collect user data.

The optional AI interpretation feature sends your card reading data (card names and positions only — no personal data) to Anthropic's Claude API for interpretation. No user identifiers are included in this request.

Data Processing

Entropy Generation Pipeline

  1. Collection — Sensor data sampled in real-time
  2. Hashing — Immediately processed through SHA-256 cryptographic hash
  3. Discarding — Raw sensor data destroyed after hashing
  4. Generation — Hash seeds HMAC-DRBG random number generator
  5. Shuffling — Fisher-Yates shuffle produces card ordering
  6. Cleanup — Entropy seed cleared from memory after shuffle

Data Storage & Retention

  • Entropy Data — Discarded immediately after processing (never retained)
  • Saved Readings — Stored locally until you delete them
  • Session Data — Cleared when you close the application
  • Server Storage — None. No user data is ever stored on any server.

Your Controls

  • Deny all sensor permissions — the app works with touch-only entropy
  • Revoke permissions at any time via your device settings
  • Delete all saved readings from the Saved Readings page
  • Clear all app data via your browser/device storage settings

Children's Privacy

Entropic Tarot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Data Subject Rights

Since we collect no personal data, most data subject rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) are not applicable. All data resides on your device under your control. You can delete it at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Material changes will be noted with an updated "Last Updated" date.

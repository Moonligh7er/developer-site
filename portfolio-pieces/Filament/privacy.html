Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Filament ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile applications, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for account identification, authentication, and communication
  • Display name — chosen by you, used within the Service
  • Password — stored only as a salted bcrypt hash; we never store plaintext passwords
  • Two-factor authentication secrets — encrypted and stored securely if you enable 2FA

1.2 Content You Submit for Analysis

When you use our analysis tools, you may submit text, URLs, or other content for processing. This includes:

  • Text submitted for persuasion analysis, content generation, or strategy design
  • Campaign configurations and parameters
  • OSINT query terms and search parameters
  • Conversation scripts and roleplay sessions

We process this content solely to provide the Service to you. See Section 3 for details on AI processing.

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about how you interact with the Service:

  • Device information — device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage patterns — features used, frequency of use, session duration
  • Error and crash data — collected via Sentry for debugging and stability improvements
  • IP address — collected for security purposes (rate limiting, abuse prevention) and approximate geolocation

1.4 Payment Information

If you subscribe to our Pro tier, payment information is collected and processed by our payment processors (Stripe and/or RevenueCat). We do not store your full credit card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment details on our servers. We receive only a transaction identifier and subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service — process your analysis requests, generate content, and deliver features
  • Maintain your account — authenticate you, manage your subscription, and store your preferences
  • Improve the Service — analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve features, performance, and user experience
  • Ensure security — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Communicate with you — send service-related notifications, security alerts, and (with your consent) product updates
  • Comply with legal obligations — respond to legal process and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your content to train AI models. We do not serve targeted advertising.

3. AI Processing

Filament uses artificial intelligence to power many of its analysis and generation features. It is important that you understand how this works:

3.1 Anthropic's Claude API

When you use AI-powered features, your submitted content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. This means:

  • Your text is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for inference (generating a response)
  • Anthropic processes the text according to their privacy policy and API terms of service
  • Under Anthropic's API terms, content submitted via the API is not used to train their models
  • We include system-level instructions with your requests but do not share your account information or identity with Anthropic

3.2 Computed vs. AI-Generated Results

The Service clearly distinguishes between:

  • Computed metrics — statistical analyses, NLP results, network graph calculations, and other deterministic computations performed on our servers. These do not involve AI inference.
  • AI-generated interpretations — narrative analysis, strategic recommendations, and natural language outputs generated by the Claude API.

Both types of output are labeled in the interface so you always know what is math and what is model.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously and employ multiple layers of protection:

MeasureDetails
Encryption at restSensitive data fields are encrypted using AES-256-GCM with unique initialization vectors
Password hashingPasswords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12. Key derivation uses PBKDF2 where applicable
Encryption in transitAll communications use TLS 1.2+

Audit trailsSecurity-relevant actions are logged with classification levels for accountability

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
  • Analysis content you submit is stored to provide history and continuity features. You may delete individual analyses or all stored content at any time.
  • Usage logs are retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes, then automatically purged.
  • Error/crash reports sent to Sentry are retained according to Sentry's data retention policies (typically 90 days).
  • Backup data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days after deletion from the live system.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format. You can export your data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

6.2 Correction

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data. You can update most account information directly in the app.

6.3 Deletion (Right to Erasure)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account through the app settings, which will trigger deletion of your personal data within 30 days. This right is subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal obligations to retain data).

6.4 Restriction and Objection

You have the right to request restriction of processing or object to certain types of processing of your personal data.

6.5 GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are in the EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal bases for processing are: contract performance (providing the Service), legitimate interest (security and improvement), and consent (where applicable). You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6.6 CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only. These are strictly necessary for the operation of the Service and include:

  • Session cookies — to maintain your authenticated session
  • CSRF tokens — to protect against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • Preference cookies — to remember your settings (e.g., theme, language)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. We do not participate in cross-site tracking or retargeting.

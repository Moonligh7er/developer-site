About Forked Tongue

We believe the best way to learn a language is through relationship, not curriculum. Forked Tongue gives you a friend who speaks the language you want to learn — with a personality, cultural knowledge, and the patience to help you grow.

What we do

Forked Tongue is an AI-powered language learning app where you learn by having real conversations with AI friends. Each character has a unique personality, cultural background, and communication style. They speak Korean, Japanese, Spanish, Portuguese, Mandarin, Cantonese, and English — and they are waiting to talk to you.

Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to sharpen your skills, your AI friend adapts to your level. They will correct you when needed, teach you slang, explain cultural nuances, and sometimes just chat about life.

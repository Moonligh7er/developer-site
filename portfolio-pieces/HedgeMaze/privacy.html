Privacy Policy

HedgeMaze — by Moonlit Social Labs

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This policy describes what information HedgeMaze collects, how it is stored, and who can access it. We built the game to require as little personal data as possible, and we intend to keep it that way.

Who We Are

HedgeMaze is developed and published by Moonlit Social Labs (Zachery McCloskey). If you have questions about this policy, reach out at moonligh7er@proton.me.

What the Game Is

HedgeMaze is a multiplayer hedge maze game available on the web and as a mobile app. Players navigate procedurally generated mazes, collect items, and compete in real-time matches. No account registration is required to play.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The game saves the following information to your browser's localStorage. This data lives entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated otherwise.

  • Game progression — XP totals, player level, mastery ratings, unlocked achievements, and gameplay statistics
  • Player preferences — audio and display settings, key bindings, selected cosmetics and themes
  • Match history and high scores — records of past games, completion times, and personal bests
  • Challenge and season progress — status of active challenges, seasonal objectives, and related counters
You own this data. It stays on your device until you clear your browser storage or uninstall the app. We cannot access it remotely.

Data Handled by the Multiplayer Server

When you join an online match, the game server processes certain information in real time to make multiplayer work:

  • Player position and game actions — movement, item usage, and interactions during the active match
  • Room and lobby state — player count, game mode, map configuration for the current session

This data is ephemeral. It exists only for the duration of a match and is discarded when the room closes. We do not persist match data on the server, maintain player profiles, or store replays.

The game server does not collect accounts, passwords, email addresses, IP logs, or any form of personal identification.

Third-Party Services

HedgeMaze integrates the following third-party services, each of which has its own data practices:

  • AppLovin MAX (advertising) — displays ads within the game. AppLovin may collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, and ad interaction data according to their own policies. Review their full privacy policy at applovin.com/privacy.
  • Optional analytics endpoint — if an analytics URL is configured at build time, the game may send anonymized gameplay events (such as match completions and feature usage counts). These events contain no personal identifiers. If no endpoint is configured, no analytics data leaves your device.

Cookies

HedgeMaze does not use cookies. All client-side storage is handled through the browser's localStorage API. Third-party ad providers may set their own cookies or use similar tracking technologies within ad content.

Children's Privacy

HedgeMaze is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the game requires no account creation and stores data only on-device, there is no mechanism through which a child could submit personal information to us.

Data Retention

  • Local data — persists until you clear your browser or app storage, or uninstall the application.
  • Server data — retained only for the duration of an active match. Once the room closes, all associated data is deleted.
  • Ad network data — retained by AppLovin according to their own retention policies.

Your Rights and Choices

Because we do not maintain accounts or store personal data on our servers, exercising your data rights is straightforward:

  • Open your browser's developer tools or app settings and clear localStorage to remove all game data from your device.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored information.
  • There is no server-side account to request deletion of.

If you have questions about data held by AppLovin through ad interactions, contact them directly through the link provided above.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this privacy policy as the game evolves. When changes occur, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the revision. Continued use of the game after a revision constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

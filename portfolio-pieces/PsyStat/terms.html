2. Description of Service

PsyStat Nexus is an AI-powered behavioral science statistics and research platform that provides:

  • Statistical Analysis Tools — including descriptive statistics, inferential tests (t-tests, ANOVA, chi-square, regression, and more), effect size calculations, power analyses, and advanced methods such as Bayesian analysis, structural equation modeling, and multilevel modeling.
  • Psychometric Analysis — reliability analysis (Cronbach's alpha, McDonald's omega), factor analysis (exploratory and confirmatory), item response theory, and scale development tools.
  • AI-Powered Interpretation — natural language explanations of statistical results, study design recommendations, methodology suggestions, and research guidance powered by large language models.
  • Research Planning Tools — study design assistance, sample size estimation, protocol templates, and curriculum tracking.
  • Specialized Modules — domain-specific statistical tools for genomics, criminology, biometrics, social network analysis, medical statistics, cognitive science, and ethnography.

Safety

Serendipity facilitates encounters with people nearby. While we build safety into the architecture, meeting strangers always carries inherent risk. Please exercise caution.

Safety Guidelines

  • Meet in public, well-lit spaces — especially for first encounters. Coffee shops, libraries, and co-working spaces are great choices.
  • Tell a trusted person — let a friend or family member know who you are meeting and where.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, leave. Your safety is more important than any connection score.
  • Use Report and Block — if someone makes you uncomfortable, report them immediately. Our trust and safety team reviews every report.

Serendipity is not responsible for the actions of its users. You assume all risk associated with in-person meetings facilitated through the App.

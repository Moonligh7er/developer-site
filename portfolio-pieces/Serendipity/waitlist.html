Frequently Asked Questions

Serendipity is a proximity-based social discovery app that uses Bluetooth Low Energy to find compatible people within 50 meters. It matches you based on shared interests, skills, and energy — not photos or profiles. Think of it as a radar for like-minded people in your physical space. Explore the protocol.

Zero-knowledge architecture means your location never leaves your device. All communications are end-to-end encrypted using X25519 key exchange, and your beacon ID rotates every 15 minutes to prevent tracking. No central server ever knows where you are. Read more about our privacy architecture.

Serendipity is currently in active development. Waitlist members receive early access before the public launch, priority placement in the mesh, and the opportunity to lock in Founder pricing at $99.99 for lifetime access to all features. Limited to first 1,000 users.

The core experience is always free (Wanderer tier with 3 pings per day).

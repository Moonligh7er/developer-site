Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Reclaim is a privacy-first application. We built it to protect your data, not exploit it. This policy explains what data we handle, how it stays secure, and your rights over it.

Reclaim processes only the personal information you explicitly provide for the purpose of scanning data brokers and generating removal requests. This may include:

  • Full name and any known aliases
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses (current and former)
  • Date of birth

We do not collect analytics, telemetry, device fingerprints, advertising identifiers, or any data beyond what you type into the app.

All personal data is stored locally on your device, encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption.

  • Your data never leaves your device in unencrypted form.
  • Encryption keys are derived from your device credentials and are never transmitted.
  • No cloud backups of your personal data are made by Reclaim.
  • You can export or delete all stored data at any time from the app settings.

Reclaim follows a local-first architecture. Your data is never sold, shared, rented, or transmitted to any party for any purpose other than executing the specific actions you initiate (e.g., checking if your email appears in a data breach).

When you run a scan or submit a removal request, only the minimum data necessary is sent to the relevant service, and only at your explicit direction.

Reclaim interacts with the following third-party services when you initiate a scan or search:

•
Have I Been Pwned (HIBP) — checks if your email appears in known data breaches. Privacy Policy
•
Google Search API — used for people-search and broker detection. Privacy Policy
•
Bing Search API — secondary search engine for broader broker coverage. Privacy Policy

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are embedded in Reclaim.

You have full control over your data within Reclaim:

  • Access — view all data Reclaim stores about you at any time from your profile.
  • Deletion — permanently delete all stored data with a single action in settings. This is immediate and irreversible.
  • Portability — export your data in a standard JSON format for use with other services.
  • Correction — edit any stored personal information at any time.

Reclaim retains data only as long as you choose. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in complete control of its lifecycle.

  • There are no mandatory retention periods imposed by Reclaim.
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.
  • You can selectively delete individual records or wipe everything at once.

Reclaim is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through Reclaim, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know — you may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected.
  • Right to Delete — you may request deletion of your personal information (already available via app settings).
  • Right to Opt-Out — Reclaim does not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Non-Discrimination — exercising your CCPA rights will not result in different pricing or service levels.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Lawful Basis — we process your data based on your explicit consent and legitimate interest in protecting your privacy.
  • Data Minimization — we only process data you explicitly provide and only for the stated purpose.
  • Right to Erasure — delete all your data at any time through the app.
  • Data Portability — export your data in machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — you may object to any processing at any time by simply not using the relevant feature.

No data is transferred outside your device, so cross-border transfer provisions do not apply.

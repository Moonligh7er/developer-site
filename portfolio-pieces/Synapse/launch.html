THE PROBLEM

Every tool you’re using is architecturally broken

They capture noise and call it signal.

Social Media

ENGAGEMENT THROUGH CONFLICT

Algorithms surface the most divisive 30% of opinion because outrage drives clicks. The 70% of latent agreement is invisible.

Surveys & Polls

LOW-FIDELITY SIGNAL

A thumbs-up, a 1–5 rating, a yes/no vote. These flatten the richness of human values into noise. A “No” could mean a dozen different things.

Town Halls

ACCESS-BIASED TESTIMONY

Biased toward people with time, confidence, and access. The loudest voice wins, not the most representative one.

“Most disagreements are actually the same underlying need expressed through different cultural vocabularies.”

“Defund the police” and “Back the blue” both contain people who want safe neighborhoods with accountable public servants. Synapse finds that signal.

HOW IT WORKS

Three transformations. One consensus.

01

The Interview

HIGH-FIDELITY SIGNAL CAPTURE

Users converse with an AI mediator trained in Motivational Interviewing and Nonviolent Communication. No surveys. No polls. A real conversation that extracts underlying values — not just surface positions. Cognitive biases are detected and gently counteracted in real time.

A “No” on a bike lane becomes: “Supports cycling infrastructure conditionally. Requires: parking mitigation. Core value: economic vitality.”
02

The Synthesis

CONSENSUS CRYSTALLIZATION

Thousands of interviews are processed through semantic clustering — grouping responses not by for/against, but by underlying value similarity. The algorithm finds what people agree on when you look at values instead of vocabulary. Bayesian confidence scoring tells you exactly how certain each finding is.

A policy simulator lets officials compose proposals and see predicted support shift in real time. “Add a trial period: +28%. Add parking alternatives: +25%.”
03

The Action

ACCOUNTABILITY & ADVOCACY

The output isn’t a report that sits on a shelf. It’s a living system: testimony scripts for council meetings, petition generators, FOIA request letters, governance scorecards, campaign finance correlation, voter guides, and real-time monitoring alerts. From insight to action in one platform.

Every artifact is auto-generated from your data with copy/download buttons. Walk into a public hearing with a data-backed testimony script and counter-arguments ready.

THE SCIENCE

Not vibes. Peer-reviewed methodology.

Every module is grounded in established research.

Motivational Interviewing

1,500+ clinical trials. The gold standard for eliciting authentic self-disclosure.

Schwartz Value Theory

Validated cross-culturally in 82 countries. 10 universal value types.

Bayesian Statistics

Credible intervals, not p-values. You know exactly how certain each finding is.

Toulmin Argumentation

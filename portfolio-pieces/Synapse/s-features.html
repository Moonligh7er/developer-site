Community Listening

How the system hears what your community actually thinks

AI-Mediated Interviews

Instead of filling out a survey, you have a real conversation with an AI mediator. It asks open-ended questions, listens for what you actually care about, and helps you articulate values you might not have put into words. Think of it as a wise counselor who wants to understand — not win an argument.

Motivational Interviewing (OARS) + NVC frameworks. Claude Sonnet with adaptive 3-7 turn interviews. Dynamic system prompt adjusts based on real-time confidence monitoring.

Cognitive Debiasing

The system detects common thinking traps — like overweighting a single bad experience or assuming nothing should ever change — and gently steers the conversation to help you think from angles you might have missed. It never tells you you're wrong.

8 bias types detected via regex patterns (status quo, availability, anchoring, NIMBY, loss aversion, confirmation, bandwagon, zero-sum). Debiasing prompts injected into mediator system prompt. Bias-adjusted confidence weights (floor 0.5).

Voice Input

Tap the microphone and speak. The system transcribes in real time. Makes participation accessible to people who find typing difficult or prefer talking.

Web Speech API (SpeechRecognition) with continuous mode. Graceful fallback on unsupported browsers.

Live Value Extraction

As you talk, the side panel shows your values and needs appearing in real time. Watch the AI understand you — see confidence bars grow as you explain what matters.

Parallel Claude Haiku calls after each participant turn. Returns emerging values with confidence scores. Animated via Framer Motion.

Interview Save & Resume

Close the browser and come back later. Your conversation is automatically saved. Pick up right where you left off.

Anonymous session tokens (crypto.randomUUID) in localStorage. Full state persisted to IndexedDB after each message.

Empathy Reveal

After your interview, see someone who seems to disagree with you — but actually shares your core values. A powerful moment that makes polarization feel less inevitable.

positionDivergence × valueOverlap = empathyScore. Schwartz circumplex + Moral Foundations + embedding cosine similarity. AI-generated narrative bridge.

Civic Intelligence

How raw perspectives become actionable insight

Consensus Synthesis

Thousands of conversations analyzed together — not by counting votes, but by grouping people by underlying values. Finds what they actually agree on. No survey could produce this.

16-dimensional value embeddings → DBSCAN clustering → AI consensus statements → Bayesian confidence (Beta-Binomial). Incremental mode for new perspectives.

Policy Simulator

Drag conditions into a proposal, see predicted support shift in real time. "Add a trial period: +28%. Add parking: +25%." Finds the cheapest path to consensus.

Diminishing returns model. Pareto frontier optimization. Automated quick-win/threshold/max-support recommendations.

Ask the Synthesis

Ask questions in plain English: "What do renters think?" Get instant answers with citations from the data.

NL question → Claude Haiku → structured response with typed citations. Template fallback for offline.

Real-Time Dashboard

Watch consensus form as interviews happen. A live feed shows every finding as it emerges.

Server-Sent Events. Event types: perspective_added, consensus_changed, gap_closed, threshold_crossed. 30s heartbeat.

Counterfactual Analysis

"What if we only counted people within 2 blocks?" Test assumptions about who drives consensus.

Re-runs clustering on filtered/weighted perspective subsets. Demographic filtering, date range, DQI minimum, weight multipliers.

Spatial Intelligence

Consensus on a map. Which blocks support the proposal? Where are participation gaps? Export as GeoJSON for your city's GIS.

Haversine distance, proximity weighting (gaussian/linear/inverse decay), grid heatmaps, spatial gap detection.

Cross-Community Intelligence

When your city debates bike lanes, what happened in Portland and Minneapolis? Real precedents with real outcomes.

Archetype library from deliberative democracy literature. Anomaly detection flags local divergence from patterns.

Historical Intelligence

This was debated in 2018 and 2022 too. How has support shifted? What was promised? Institutional memory that outlasts any official.

Issue recurrence detection. Consensus evolution tracking (strengthening/weakening/oscillating). Prior outcome retrieval.

Government Accountability

How governance is measured against community will

Representation Alignment

A civic credit score for officials. "Council Member X aligned 34% across 6 issues." Not partisan — just data.

Vote alignment (60%) + condition alignment (40%). Promise-keeping score. Report card with letter grades.

Budget-Value Alignment

"94% consensus on safety, 8% of budget." See where spending matches — and contradicts — community priorities.

Keyword mapping of budget line items to value priorities. Alignment gap computation. Reallocation recommendations.

Campaign Finance Correlation

Follow the money. "Officials receiving >$5K from real estate voted against housing consensus 83% of the time."

Pearson correlation between donation amounts and vote-against-consensus rate. Funded vs unfunded differential.

Transparency Scoring

Grade governance process quality across 7 dimensions. Detects potential open meetings law violations.

7 dimensions scored 0-3. Compliance flag detection for Sunshine Law violations. Improvement plans.

Governance Scorecard

One grade. Published quarterly. Representation + budget + transparency + outcomes + responsiveness + equity.

Weighted composite (25/20/15/15/15/10). Trend tracking. Shareable summary. Full methodology.

Accountability Timeline

Promise → Hearing → Vote → Implementation → Outcome. Tracks drift between promises and delivery.

13 event types, 6 phases. Drift detection. Skipped-phase identification. Process quality grading.

Corporate Accountability

Track corporate promises vs reality. "15% affordable units promised. 8% delivered." Flags broken binding commitments.

Enforceability-weighted scoring. Auto-status from target vs actual. Deadline tracking.

Predictive Model

Logistic regression. Features: donations, tenure, prior alignment, absenteeism, committees. Trainable from outcomes.

Media Narrative Gap

Per-article framing/sentiment/tone analysis. Narrative gap detection vs synthesis confidence.

Electoral Alignment

Monitoring & Alerts

Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 4, 2026

Privacy by Design

The Synapse Protocol is architecturally designed so that re-identifying individuals from synthesis output is mathematically impossible. Your interview passes through 4 layers: Interview → Anonymize → Synthesize → Output. Personal identity is stripped at the anonymization layer and never enters the synthesis engine.

What We Collect

  • Interview responses: Your conversation with the AI mediator, linked to an anonymous session token (not your identity)
  • Extracted values & needs: Anonymized structured data derived from your conversation
  • Quality metrics: Discourse Quality Index scores, cognitive bias indicators, Schwartz value classifications, argumentation structure
  • Technical data: IP address (for rate limiting only — not stored long-term), browser type, pages visited
  • Evidence submissions: Content you voluntarily submit after applying your own redactions

What We Don't Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information (unless you voluntarily provide it via contact/feedback forms)
  • Your location beyond what you voluntarily share
  • Biometric data, facial recognition, or device fingerprinting

How We Use Data

  • Synthesize community consensus (anonymized, aggregated)
  • Generate accountability analyses from public records (entered by administrators)
  • Improve interview quality through DQI and bias analysis
  • Generate advocacy materials from aggregate data

We Don't Sell Your Data

Your data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for profiling. Revenue comes from organizations that deploy Synapse, not from monetizing participants.

Your Rights

Via the Your Data page, you can:

  • View all data linked to your session
  • Export everything as JSON
  • Delete all data permanently (irreversible)

Previously generated anonymized syntheses are not affected by deletion — individual contributions cannot be disaggregated from aggregate outputs.

Age Requirement

Users must be at least 17 years old. Age verification occurs before any data collection. If we learn data was collected from someone under 17, we will delete it immediately.

Cookies

We use essential cookies (session management, consent state). Third-party advertising cookies may be used if ads are displayed. You can opt out via Google Ads Settings.

Third Parties

Interview content is processed by Anthropic (Claude AI) subject to their data processing agreements. We may use hosting (Vercel), analytics (Google Analytics), and error tracking (Sentry) services.

Contact

Questions about privacy: Contact page. Moonlit Social Labs.
